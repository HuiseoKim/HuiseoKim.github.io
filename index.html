About Me

Hello! I'm Huiseo Kim, a computer science researcher specializing in natural language processing (NLP), particularly in Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) systems. My work aims to enhance multi-hop retrieval, question answering, and knowledge-based reasoning using cutting-edge techniques such as reinforcement learning and representation learning. I'm currently pursuing my studies at Yonsei University, Seoul.
